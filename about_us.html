OUR STORY

Fareed & Narayanan founded Angivest Ventures in June 2017 with the purpose of investing in early stage ventures globally. They interviewed well-over 150+ founders and entrepreneurs across various geographies including US, Europe, Middle East & Asia, and understood the pain points founders go through in their fundraising campaign. On the other hand, being seasoned investors themselves, they understood the complexities in performing diligence on investee companies with the “early stage” investor mindset. As early stage investors, Fareed & Narayanan have a strong appreciation of how long it takes to get your money back! To solve for all the above challenges, they created Bambucorn.

WHAT WE DO ?

We are an innovative equity crowdfunding platform founded with the purpose to help ambitious entrepreneurs raise as little as $100,000 for their ventures and scale by providing an early use platform for investors to invest as little as $1,000. We let everyone participate in the venture world.
