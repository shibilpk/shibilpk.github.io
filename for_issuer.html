ISSUER

We, at Bambucorn, realize how challenging it can be for entrepreneurs to raise funding. We help you build a narrative, create winning pitch decks, and let you focus on your core operations. In addition, studies have shown that crowdfunding platforms help entrepreneurs:

  • Increase Customer Acquisition
  • Improve Brand Loyalty
  • Access A Broad Investor Base

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST TO RAISE CAPITAL ON BAMBUCORN?

NO UPFRONT COST

Bambucorn only charges you if your offering is successful. Our charges are detailed in theIssuer FAQs.

WHAT ARE WE LOOKING FOR?

  1. Disruptive Technologies – Your idea doesn’t have to be entirely new, it may create an incremental improvement in current processes making the ultimate use of the product cheaper, quicker, or more convenient.

  2. Traction – This could be in the form of number of customers or any other metric that demonstrates user engagement.

  3. People – Passionate founders who are hungry to succeed.

FUNDRAISING PROCESS

  • Step 1:

    Apply via our application portal: Fill out a questionnaire answering details about various aspects of your business.

  • Step 2:

    Due Diligence: Our team will review your application and respond with a few questions/suggestions. We aim to provide you our approval or otherwise, with a feedback within two weeks (of course, this depends on the quality of the initial application and the promptness of your response to our questions)

  • Step 3:

    List your company on the platform. We are in business!
